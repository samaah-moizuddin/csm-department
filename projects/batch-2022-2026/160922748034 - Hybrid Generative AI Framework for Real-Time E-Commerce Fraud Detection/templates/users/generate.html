Synthetic Fraud Data Generation

GAN-based data augmentation for balanced model training

Why generate synthetic data?

In standard e-commerce datasets, fraudulent transactions typically make up less than 5% of all transactions. This massive class imbalance forces AI models to become heavily biased towards predicting "Not Fraud."

By using an adversarial process (Generative Adversarial Network or GAN), we can synthesize highly realistic new fraud transaction samples to balance the dataset, leading to significantly higher predictive recall.
